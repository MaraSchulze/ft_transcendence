How to play?

🏓 Welcome to Classic Pong! 🏓

Get ready for the ultimate battle of reflexes in this timeless arcade classic! Keep your eyes on the ball and may the best paddle win!

🎮 Challenger Controls

O Move Up
L Move Down

🎮 Opponent Controls

W Move Up
S Move Down

First to score 3 points wins!
